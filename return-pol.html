Returns Policy

1. Eligibility for Returns

Items must be returned within 30 days of the purchase date.

The product must be in original condition, unused, and in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and tags.

Proof of purchase, such as the original receipt or order confirmation, is required.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are not eligible for return:

Products that are customized or personalized.

Items marked as final sale at the time of purchase.

Products that show signs of damage, misuse, or alteration.

3. How to Return an Item

Initiate a Return:

Contact our Customer Support team at support@bang-olufsen.com or call +1-800-BANG-OLUFSEN to request a return.

Provide your order details and reason for return.

Pack the Item:

Ensure the product is securely packaged to prevent damage during transit.

Include all original accessories and proof of purchase in the package.

Ship the Return:

Use the pre-paid return shipping label provided by our support team.

Drop off the package at the specified shipping carrier or schedule a pickup.

5. Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for defective or damaged items. Contact our support team within 14 days of receiving the item to initiate an exchange.

6. Warranty Claims

If your product develops a fault outside the 30-day return window but within the warranty period, you may be eligible for repair or replacement under our Warranty Policy. Contact our support team for assistance.

7. International Returns

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a product defect or error in the order.
